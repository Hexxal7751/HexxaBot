Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Introduction

HexxaBot is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our Discord bot. By using HexxaBot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collected

When you interact with HexxaBot, the following data may be collected and stored:

  • User IDs - To track game progress, statistics, and leaderboards
  • Server IDs - To manage server-specific settings and data
  • Game Data - Scores, progress, and statistics for all games
  • Direct Message (DM) Interactions - The bot may send you DMs for game invites, gameplay, or notifications. No DM content is stored, but game actions may be logged for stats.

As new games and features are added, additional data types may be collected. These will always be used solely for bot functionality and never for advertising or third-party sharing.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is securely stored in Supabase, a trusted cloud database service. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Data is only accessible to authorized personnel involved in the development and maintenance of HexxaBot.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To track game progress and statistics for each user and server
  • To create and maintain leaderboards within Discord servers
  • To enable DM-based features such as game invitations and notifications
  • To improve user experience and bot functionality
  • To identify users who have left servers for data cleanup purposes

5. Data Retention

Game-related data is stored until one of the following occurs:

  • You leave the Discord server (data is automatically purged during cleanup)
  • A server administrator runs the data purge command
  • You explicitly request data removal (contact the bot owner or use available commands)

6. Third-Party Services

HexxaBot does not share, sell, or distribute user data to third parties. Data is used solely for bot functionality and stored exclusively in Supabase. No advertising or analytics services are used.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of using the bot at any time
  • Contact the bot owner for any privacy concerns

8. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time to reflect new features or changes in data handling. Continued use of the bot after policy changes indicates your acceptance of the updated terms. The latest version is always available on the bot's website.
