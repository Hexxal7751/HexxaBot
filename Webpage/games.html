Game Guide

Master every game HexxaBot offers! Each section below details the objective, rules, and helpful slash-commands so your community can jump in without confusion.

🎮 Rock Paper Scissors

Play the timeless classic against HexxaBot. Simple yet addicting—perfect for quick duels during chat!

  • Objective: Outsmart the bot by choosing a move that beats theirs.
  • Gameplay: Initiate with /rps. The bot will present interactive buttons so you can pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Statistics: HexxaBot records total wins, losses, and win-rate for each user per server.
/rps
/rps-stats [user]
/rps-lb

Tips to Master RPS:

  • Watch for patterns in your bot's moves
  • Avoid repeating the same move multiple times
  • Try to think one step ahead of the bot
  • Use psychology - bot may subconsciously avoid repeating losses

🔢 Guess Number

Put your intuition to the test by guessing a secret number between 1-100 in 10 tries or fewer.

  • Objective: Reveal the hidden number with the fewest guesses.
  • Gameplay: Use /guess-num. After each guess, HexxaBot reveals whether the target is higher or lower.
  • Scoring: Your accuracy percentage is tracked and showcased on the leaderboard.
/guess-num
/guess-num-stats [user]
/guess-num-lb

Tips to Master Guess Number:

  • Start with 50 to quickly narrow down the range
  • Use binary search strategy - always guess the middle of the remaining range
  • Pay attention to the hints - "higher" or "lower"
  • Try to minimize the number of guesses by making strategic choices

⭕ Tic Tac Toe

Challenge friends in a polished, button-based Tic Tac Toe board directly in chat.

  • Objective: Be the first to align three symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Gameplay: Start a match with /tictactoe @opponent. Players take turns by clicking board-square buttons.
  • Fair Play: A 30-second turn timer ensures fast-paced games.
/tictactoe @user

Tips to Master Tic Tac Toe:

  • Take the center square if available - it gives you the most winning opportunities
  • Control the corners - they're part of three potential winning lines
  • Look for two-in-a-row opportunities to win
  • Block your opponent when they have two-in-a-row
  • Force your opponent into positions where they must block you instead of creating their own opportunities

⚔️ Battle

A turn-based RPG mini-game with abilities, critical hits, and multiple modes (PvP only).

  • Objective: Reduce your opponent's HP to zero using strategic attacks and heals.
  • Gameplay: Trigger with /battle @opponent to challenge another player.
  • Modes: Normal (default), No Healing (limited heals), Poison (damage over time), Regen (heal over time), Stun (chance to stun opponent).
  • Abilities: Punch, Kick, Defend, Heal (limited). Critical hits and misses add unpredictability!
  • Progression: Wins, losses, and damage dealt are tracked for bragging rights.
/battle @user [mode]
/battle-lb

Tips to Master Battle:

  • Use Defend when your opponent is likely to attack
  • Save Heal for critical moments when your HP is low
  • Try different game modes for varied strategies
  • Watch your opponent's patterns to predict their next move

🃏 Flip & Find

A memory-matching card game. Turn cards to find pairs before your opponent does!

  • Objective: Collect the most matching pairs on a shared grid.
  • Gameplay: Run /flipnfind @opponent or play solo against the clock.
  • Twist: ⭐Star cards award bonus points; mismatches pass the turn.
/flipnfind [@user]
/flipnfind-stats [user]
/flipnfind-lb

Tips to Master Flip & Find:

  • Memorize the positions of cards as they're revealed
  • Focus on matching pairs you've already seen
  • Try to remember the locations of star cards for bonus points
  • Plan your moves ahead to maximize consecutive matches
  • Don't rush - take your time to think about the best moves

🎩 The Kidnapped Jack

An exciting card-drawing party game inspired by the classic Old Maid.

  • Objective: Avoid holding the single Jack card when the deck runs out.
  • Gameplay: Start with /kidnapped-jack. Up to 6 players can join within 2 minutes.
  • Turns: Draw one random card from the next player each turn. Automatic pair removals keep the pace brisk.
  • Victory: First player eliminated (no cards left) wins. Last player holding the Jack is the kidnapper and is the Loser.
  • Strategy: Think before you click! The first player eliminated wins!
/kidnapped-jack
/kidnapped-jack-stats [user]
/kidnapped-jack-lb

Tips to Master The Kidnapped Jack:

  • Think before you click - the first player eliminated wins the match!
  • Try to get rid of pairs quickly to reduce your card count
  • Pay attention to how many cards other players have
  • Strategically choose which player to draw from
  • Remember that being the Kidnapper is actually losing the game!
